Templates & RFQs

📥 Downloading a Free Template

1. Click the grey "Download Template (FREE)" button on any card.

2. Fill in your name, phone, email, and location.

3. Optionally add your budget range, timeline, and any certifications needed.

4. Click "Authorize Request & Download Template".

✓ You'll receive the template and be connected to local pros in that category.

💳 Buying a Paid Asset

1. Click the amber "Buy Asset ($XX)" button.

2. Review the asset name and price in the popup.

3. Click "Checkout via Stripe" to complete payment securely.

Paid assets include detailed Excel templates, contract shells, compliance guides, and audit packets.

📬 Submitting an RFQ

1. Click the blue "Dispatch RFQ" button on any service card.

2. Fill in your contact info, location, budget range, and timeline.

3. Add any certification requirements (e.g. OSHA 30, Bonded, MBE).

4. Click "Securely Dispatch RFQ to Pro Network".

✓ Verified professionals in your area follow up via phone or email.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are the business listings real companies?

Live listings (tagged with a city/state) are real businesses sourced from OpenStreetMap. Static niche entries are curated service categories with templated scope info.

How do I find contractors in my specific city?

Select your state and city from the geographic console dropdowns. Only listings matching your area will appear in the directory.

What's the difference between free and paid resources?

Free resources are general-use templates and checklists. Paid assets ($19–$199) are professionally formatted scope templates, contract shells, and compliance matrices designed to save hours of work.

What happens after I submit an RFQ?

Your project details are routed to the ServiceScopeHQ pro network. Verified contractors in your area and service category will follow up via phone or email.

Is my information secure?

All form submissions are processed through Netlify's secure infrastructure. Payment checkout is handled by Stripe (PCI-DSS compliant). Your data is never sold to third parties.
